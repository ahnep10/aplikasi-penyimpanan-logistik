<feature>
  <name>FIFO Inventory Reservation</name>
  <files>app/Services/InventoryService.php, tests/Unit/FIFOAllocationTest.php</files>
  <behavior>
    - When allocating stock for an order item, the system must select batches of the product where `current_quantity > 0`.
    - Batches must be processed in order of `received_at` (ascending).
    - For each batch, it must decrement `current_quantity` and create an `InventoryMovement` (outbound) with `batch_id`.
    - It must continue until the full requested quantity is allocated or all batches are exhausted.
  </behavior>
  <implementation>
    Create `InventoryService` with `allocateFIFO(Order $order)` method. Use `lockForUpdate()` on batches during the process.
  </implementation>
</feature>
